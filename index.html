EDM Library

The EDM Library is a sealed cabinet of valuable resources for students of the EDM Department. We offer books ranging from reference to typography and history. The size of our library largely depends on donations by students and faculty. If you know of anyone or you are someone with design books that you want to get off your hands, please contact the EDM Club officers so that we may add them to the library.

An internship questionaire is available on the EDM Website. You can record your skill level in each category so that professors can match you to internships (paid/ unpaid/ credit) when they get emails from companies seeking interns. To fill out the internship questionaire (a nonbinding form) go to the Electronic Design and Multimedia Website
